Front Sights

Marble Arms front sights are machined from solid steel and built to survive real-world use. Available in fiber optic, tritium, and traditional bead styles to fit a wide range of popular firearms.

Drop-in fitment ensures a clean install with no gunsmithing required.

Rear Sights

Our rear sights deliver a crisp, repeatable sight picture across pistol and rifle platforms. Fixed and adjustable models are available, engineered to maintain zero under heavy recoil.

Built from the same all-steel stock that has defined Marble Arms quality for over 130 years.

Peep Sights

Peep tang sights offer a classic, precision aiming solution preferred by hunters and competition shooters alike. Marble Arms peep sights feature micro-adjustable apertures for windage and elevation.

Machined to tight tolerances for a solid, rattle-free fit on lever-action and bolt-action rifles.

Shotgun Sights

Marble Arms shotgun sights give wing shooters and slug hunters a reliable aiming reference without obstructing the field of view. Available in standard bead, fiber optic bead, and mid-rib configurations.

Easy to install and built to handle the punishing recoil of field-grade shotguns.

Accessories

Complete your sight system with Marble Arms accessories including fiber optic rods, height gauges, sight tools, and installation hardware.

Every accessory is designed to work seamlessly with our sight lineup. Purpose-built tools make installation and adjustment fast and accurate.
