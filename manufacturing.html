OEM & Manufacturing

Partnering with America's firearms manufacturers since 1892.

A trusted partner.
Not just a supplier.

For over 130 years, America's top firearms manufacturers have relied on Marble Arms for precision-machined components that perform to exact specification—every run, every time. We don't ship parts; we deliver on a commitment.

Our engineering team works directly with OEM partners from initial design through production validation. When tolerances are tight and quality standards are non-negotiable, Marble Arms is the call that gets made first.

What We Run

Machining Capabilities

CNC Multi-Axis Turning

Live tooling and multi-axis capability for complex rotational components. Tight tolerances held across production volumes.

CNC Milling

3 and 4-axis milling for prismatic parts. Consistent surface finishes and dimensional accuracy across high-volume runs.

Surface & Cylindrical Grinding

Precision grinding for components requiring sub-thousandth tolerances and fine surface finish specifications.

EDM

Wire and sinker EDM for intricate features and hardened materials where conventional cutting is not practical.

Threading & Broaching

Internal and external threading to spec. Broaching for keyways, splines, and non-round internal profiles.

Finishing & Plating

Bluing, parkerizing, and protective coatings coordinated through qualified finishing partners to meet your specifications.

PPAP & Manufacturing Processes

We support full Production Part Approval Process (PPAP) submissions at all levels. Our APQP-aligned process ensures that quality is designed in before the first production part ships—not inspected in after the fact.

Control plans, FMEAs, and measurement system analyses are developed and maintained throughout the product lifecycle. We don't walk away after first article approval.

1 Advanced Product Quality Planning (APQP)
2 Control Plans
3 FMEA Development
4 Production Validation
5 Ongoing Process Monitoring

PPAP Levels 1–5 supported. We'll work with your quality team to determine the appropriate submission level and documentation package for your program requirements.

Zero-Defect Expectation

Quality & SPC

CMM Inspection

Coordinate measuring machine inspection for critical dimensions. Full dimensional reports available as part of PPAP and ongoing production validation.

Statistical Process Control

SPC monitoring on key characteristics throughout production runs. Control charts and Cpk data available to demonstrate process capability and stability.

First Article Inspection

Comprehensive first article inspection reports (FAIR/PPAP) before production release. Every characteristic on the print is measured and documented.

From Drawing to Part

Engineering Capabilities

We Work From Your Drawings

Submit your CAD files or 2D drawings and we'll quote from them directly. Our machinists are fluent in GD&T and understand how print tolerances translate to real-world machining requirements.

  • — GD&T interpretation and DFM feedback
  • — Tight tolerances to ±0.0005" and tighter
  • — Accepted formats: STEP, IGES, SolidWorks, DXF, PDF
  • — NDA available prior to drawing submission

We Can Draw It For You

Don't have drawings yet? Our in-house engineering team can take your concept, sample part, or performance requirement and develop a complete print package ready for production.

  • — In-house engineering and 3D modeling
  • — Rapid prototyping and design iteration
  • — Reverse engineering from sample parts
  • — Design-for-manufacturability built into every print

Ready to discuss your OEM requirements?

Our team is ready to review your drawings, discuss tolerances, and put together a program that works for your production schedule.
